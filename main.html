Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can perform an almost impossible task: inception, planting an idea into someone’s mind.
