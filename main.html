Summary

Inception is a science-fiction thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. He is offered a chance to erase his criminal history by performing the impossible task of planting an idea in someone’s mind.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Elliot Page, and Tom Hardy. Their performances bring emotional depth and intensity to the complex storyline.
